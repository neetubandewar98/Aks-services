AWS SysOps

SysOps (system operator) is an administrator handling multiple computer-systems connected virtually. The administrators have privileges of monitoring, reviewing and providing necessary permissions for task orientation. It also secures confidential data.

Why AWS SysOps?

  • Operate automatable and error-free systems and networks on AWS.
  • Control & monitor the data transfer.
  • Be a certified AWS SysOps Administrator.

What you’ll master

  • Creating virtually private-networks.
  • Understanding of different networking concepts (like firewalls)
